## Preface

I should mention this at first. Honestly speaking, I am not good at organic chemistry at all. In 2019, I took organic chemistry class in both spring and fall semester. However, knowledge was very volatile: a few years later, I can barely remember what I learned. While I am taking a year off the university, I decided to review undergraduate-level stuffs that I have learned. Therefore, my posts will be concentrated on the reactions I want (or need) to review.
