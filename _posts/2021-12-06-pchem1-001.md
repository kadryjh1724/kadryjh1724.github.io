## Preface

Thermodynamics is a composite word of *thermo* (~heat) and *dynamics* (~flow). Thermodynamics is based on macroscopic, empirical laws which have nothing to do with microscopic details. Actually, it is kind of weird to put "dynamics" in thermodynamics, because usually the term thermodynamics describes equilibrium states - they do not evolve along the time - which are **not** dynamic. At first, we learn the law of thermodynamics and its consequences. After that, we will consider connection with microscopic, molecular level phenomenons.
